Hi, I am Huascar (pronounced wäs-kär), a CS PhD candidate working under the guidance of Prof. Jim Whitehead, and part of the Software Introspection Lab at UC Santa Cruz.

My core research area is software engineering, with a focus on source code curation and search-driven development.

The goal of my research is to ensure the code snippets programmers find on the Web are suitable and trustworthy. To achieve this, I study the above areas with the aim of inventing technologies that allow programmers to clean, annotate, and refine (i.e., curate) code snippets found; before their actual consumption in the IDEs.

Please feel free to e-mail me if you have any questions about my research and current projects. Thank you for visiting.
